A Square London (ASL) is an Entertainment & Fashion Management organization launched in 2013 in London by Fashion Designers Akhil & Aiswarya. ASL produces Fashion Weeks, Fashion Reality Shows, Top Model Shows, International Fashion and Lifestyle Awards & Trade Fairs. ASL focused to showcase and exhibit creative talents in the Fashion industry from across the globe on a single platform.  Through its global connection, ASL will provide not only the finest brands and international designers to exhibit and showcase their creative art, but also introduce SME’s and YOUNG EMERGING TALENTS to market their innovative designs around the globe. ‘A Square London’ was established in London and it has been executing Fashion Shows, Reality Shows, and Fashion & Lifestyle awards with global strength. Our team comprises highly Experienced & Talented Professionals and more importantly people with a Vision, and, one Goal to make the World a smaller place. Smaller in terms of Distance, Culture, Food, and Creativity. “We bring the World Together” through our different projects in various countries.
