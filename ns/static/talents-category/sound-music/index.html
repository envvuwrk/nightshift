Our Sound+Music talent

Nightshift partners with Benzene's talented pool of producers and artists to create original, tailor-made soundtracks that bring your projects to vibrant life. We provide in-house services for score, sound effects and voiceover recording in our three custom-built recording studios. In addition, we have extensive experience in VR audio integration, including ambisonic recording and binaural reproduction.
